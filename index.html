About Me

Professional Photo Placeholder

I am a Geospatial Analyst and researcher passionate about leveraging spatial technology to address climate challenges and build resilient communities across Africa. As the first female to graduate with First Class Honours in the history of my department at Obafemi Awolowo University, I have dedicated my career to breaking barriers and creating impact through geospatial innovation.

My work spans disaster preparedness, flood risk assessment, and community-driven mapping initiatives. From leading teams that mapped over 5,000 buildings in flood-vulnerable settlements to developing real-time flood impact assessments that served 1,500+ residents, I combine technical expertise with community engagement to deliver solutions that matter.

As a Global Winner of the VSCP Hackathon 2025 and published researcher at FOSS4G Europe 2025, I have demonstrated my ability to innovate at the intersection of technology and social impact.
