About Me

I am a Geospatial Analyst dedicated to transforming complex spatial data into actionable insights for climate resilience and disaster preparedness. I specialize in building end-to-end geospatial workflows‚Äîfrom remote sensing analysis and field data collection to developing interactive mapping solutions. Over the past three years, I have applied my expertise across disaster risk management, flood assessment, healthcare mapping, and land administration.

My journey includes leading teams to map 5,000+ buildings in flood-vulnerable settlements, developing real-time flood impact assessments, and co-creating LandAlert, an award-winning environmental monitoring system. As the first female First Class Honours graduate in my department's history and a Global Winner of the VSCP Hackathon 2025, I thrive at the intersection of technical innovation and social impact.
